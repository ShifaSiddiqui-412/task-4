# Kamran Tessori

Kamran Tessori is a prominent Pakistani politician and businessman who is the current Governor of Sindh. He was appointed to the position on October 9, 2022.

## Political Career

Tessori is a member of the Muttahida Qaumi Movement - Pakistan (MQM-P). His appointment as Governor of Sindh was a significant political development.

## Business Ventures

Prior to his full-time political career, Kamran Tessori was a successful businessman.
